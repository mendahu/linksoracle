Glitches/Cheats

Glitches It looks like I really wanted this to be a place where users could submit different glitches they had found, with a credit link to their email and a date stamp. It's funny to see little seeds of wanting to do more with the code but not having the skills to pull it off all the way.

I know I had been experimenting with web forms at the time, but I remember them being very difficult to understand. Knowing what I know now, I can imagine why - this website had no server running it, so I'd have had to have somewhere else to send completed forms. I think there was some service I had used which would email me the data and then I would manually add it in here.

Posted MAY 17 99 by Ganon: More than four bottles.
I know how to get more than 4 bottles. What you have to do is make sure you already have one bottle and go to Zora's Domain or anywhere else where you can catch fish in a bottle. Put the bottle to a C button and swipe at a fish. Before Link floows through on the swing, or before the fish enters the bottle, press start and replace the bottle in the C buttons with a useless item, like the biggoron Claim Check or the deku nuts, or Farore's Wind. Unpause and that item will become a bottle! You can do it to all your items, so up to 21 bottles!
