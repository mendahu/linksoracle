Young Link's Trading Sequence

Start at the Happy Mask Shop. All of the Maskes appear there after you sell the previous one so come back when you sell one. Borrow the Keaton Mask.

Keaton Mask-Guard-Kakariko Village-Z-Target him and where the mask and then talk to him. He's near the gate to Death Mountain
Skull Mask-Skull Kid-Lost Woods-Again Talk to him while wearing Mask
Spooky Mask-Graveyard Kid-Kakariko Graveyard-Same technique hear.
Bunny Hood-Running Man-Hyrule Field-Lastly, use the same technique. Talk with mask.

As a reward, Link will be able to use 1 of four masks at a time:

Mask of Truth-This mask allows Link to read minds and talk to gossip stones. It also has a few other uses
Goron Mask-This mask, along with others change what people say to you
Zora Mask-The same goes for this Mask
Gerudo Mask-And this one
