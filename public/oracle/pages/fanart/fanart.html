Fan Art


Silvestris I called this page Fan Art but it really ended up being a Silvestris tribute page. Silvestris was a talented artist who made some really great drawings of this game. I think she was around my age and I remember her being very gracious about sharing her work and letting me display it.

The links here were broken when I found them, so out of curiosity I went searching for her. I'm delighted to learn she pursued illustration and now runs her own company in Sweden.

I was able to find most of these images hosted around the web and have made copies here to preserve the connections.
