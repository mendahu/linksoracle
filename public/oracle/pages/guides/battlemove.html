Here is a section that tells you how to move Link and do his moves.

Walking-Hold the control stick slightly forward, not all the way and Link will walk slowly.

Running-If you hold the stick all the way forward, Link will run.

Jumping-When Link encounters a ledge, he will automatically jump, providing you were running straight towards that ledge.

Swimming-When link is in water deep enough, he will wade on the spot. the control stick will move in the appropriate directions. Tapping B will speed link's swimming up. Pressing and holding A will make him dive to his capable depth.

Climbing-When Link approachs a climbable surface, he will automatically climb up with the stick forward, down with the stick back, and side to side with the stick sideways. At any time, you may press A to have Link Drop downwards to the ground. On some objects you will have to press A to commence climbing.

Grabbing/Throwing-When Link approachs a grabbable object, press A when GRAB appears on the Action button. Link will pick the object up and hold it high. Pressing A again will cause Link to drop the object. If you press A while Link is running, he will throw the object a considerable distance.

Pushing/Pulling-When Link approachs a movable object, GRAB will appear on the Action Icon. Holding A and moving the stick back and forth will cause Link to push or pull the object.

Other Actions with A-Open will open chests and doors. Speak will speak to the person in front of you. Check will check any signs on signs or doors.

Combat Training

Slice-Tapping B will cause Link to slice horizontally.

Combo-Tapping B three or more times in a row will cause Link to do a variety of cuts and slices.

Stab-Pressing Z, moving your stick forward and pressing B will cause Link to stab at his enemy.

Chop-Holding Z and pressing B will cause Link to chop vertically.

Jump Attack-Holding Z and pressing A, Link will jump and bring his sword down on to his enemy with devastating force.

Spin Attack-Holding B will charge a Spin Attack. If you have Magic, It will become more powerful the longer you hold it. Releasing causes Link to Spin, hitting everything around him.

Roll Attack-Running and pressing A will cause Link to roll. If you time it right, you can avoid damage.

BackFlip-Holding Z, pressing back on the stick and A will cause Link to Back flip and miss damage.

Dodge-Holding Z and Pressing Side to side on stick with A will Cause Link to Jump sideways, avoiding Damage.

Z-Target-Tapping Z when Navi has found an enemy will cause Link to target him, never leaving him out of his sight. This also enables all combat moves.
