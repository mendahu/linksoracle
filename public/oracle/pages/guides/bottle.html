4 Bottles

Bottle #1
Go to Lon Lon Ranch as a child and go into the Chicken coop. First its best to move all the chickens and throw them behind Talon. Talk to him and pay him to play the game. Bring all the good chickens back and he'll give you a bottle.

Bottle #2
Go to Kakariko Village as a child and talk to the cucco Girl. Bring all the chickens back and stick them in the pen and she'll gve you a bottle. Here are the locations:

Near village entrance gate, walking around.
Grab the first chicken and jump from the pile of bricks to the house of skulltula, behind the gate. Throw both down and return them.
Right by the pen, one's walking around.
Before you go up Death Mountain, beside the stairs.
Main floor, in crate at bottom of big stairs.
Use the previous chicken to go up to the windmill amd jump off over the fence. Throw both back over.
Go up the ladder behind the fence and go around the windmill. Throw one off.

Bottle #3
Get the silver scale in Zora's Domain and go through the passage to Lake Hylia. In the water at the bottom lies a bottle with a message.
