Rumours I remember there being this persistent intrigue with video games on the web at this time, speculating wildly on different hidden things you could discover inside the game, no doubt fueled by real examples like the Konami Code. Lots of folks would doctor fake images claiming they'd found a new easter egg, and I guess 13 year old me felt the need to address the claims.

For this game, the big rumour was that you could find and acquire the Triforce which would yield some unknowable power. But worry not, I'm here to look out for you. Debunking conspiracy theories since 1999.
