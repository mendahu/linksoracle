Fairy Fountain Locations

Here are the locations of all the normal fairy fountains and where you can get Big Fairies.

Hyrule Field-Under a boulder near some trees, use bombs.

Kakariko Graveyard-In the grave where you get Hylian Sheild, blow up wall behind it.

Zora's River-Up top where the big boulder is. Use bombs.

Sacred Forest Meadow-Near top of maze, in grotto on ground surrounded by walls. Use bombs.

Zora's Domain-Play the song of storms on island in middle.

Gerudo Fortress-Behind pair of boxes, play song of storms.

Ganon's Castle-Under Bridge and back through wall. Use Lens of Truth.

Gossip Stones-Play the Song of Storms, Zelda's Lullaby, or Epona's Song at a Gossip Stone and a big fairy will come out.
